## 🚀 What is SkillToken?
Ever needed a quick fix for your laptop? A midnight snack? Or someone to explain that one DBMS topic 10 minutes before the exam? 

**SkillToken** is a Telegram-native marketplace built exclusively for our campus. It connects students who **need help** with students who **have skills**, all within the app you already use every day.

---

## 🔥 Why Use It?

### 1. 🧠 Smart Semantic Search
You don't need to know the exact keywords. You can type "I need help with Java" or "Who can cook for me?" and our AI-powered search understands your **intent**. It matches you with the right person even if their listing uses different words!

### 2. 🎓 College-Verified Privacy
SkillToken is locked to your campus. Students in **RKGIT** only see listings from **RKGIT**. Your listings and your searches are private to your specific college community.

### 3. 💸 Zero Commission
We don't take a cut. You talk to the helper directly via Telegram DM, sort out the time, and pay them whatever way you both like (UPI, Cash, or a Treat!).

---

## 🛠️ How to Use SkillToken

### Step 1: Set Up Your Profile
Before you can list or search, you need to tell the bot which campus you belong to.
*   Type `/profile`
*   Enter your College Name (e.g., `RKGIT Ghaziabad`)

### Step 2: Register a Skill (Earn Money!)
Have a talent? List it in seconds.
*   Type `/register`
*   **Describe the skill:** "I can set up Python/VS Code", "I make the best 2 AM Maggi", or "I can help with AutoCAD".
*   **Set your price:** Type anything! "₹50", "Free", or "Negotiable".

### Step 3: Find Help (Get it Done!)
Need something? Just ask.
*   **Direct Search:** Simply type what you need into the chat (e.g., "Need a ride to the station") and hit enter. 
*   The bot will show you a list of students in your college who can help.
*   Click their **@username** to start a chat and get it done!
